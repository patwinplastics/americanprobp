Questions

Buying American Pro
through a dealer.

The questions homeowners, builders, and lumberyards ask most about the American Pro dealer network, answered straight.

Where can I buy American Pro PVC decking?

American Pro Legacy PVC decking is stocked on Cape Cod at Stonewood Products, with a Harwich flagship (508-430-5020) and a Mashpee store (508-477-9950). TrueGrain Deck and other lines are available factory-direct from Linden, NJ or through your local lumber dealer. Call 1-877-442-6776 and we'll route your job to the closest authorized partner.

Where can I buy American Pro PVC porch flooring?

American Pro tongue-and-groove PVC porch flooring is stocked across Western New York and the Finger Lakes through Genesee Reserve Supply, with branches in Rochester (585-292-7040) and Buffalo (716-824-3116). Outside Western NY, porch flooring ships factory-direct from Linden, NJ.

Does Stonewood Products ship American Pro decking outside Cape Cod?

Yes. Stonewood Products fulfills American Pro Legacy PVC decking orders nationally through their online team at 1-855-786-9663 or stonewoodproducts.com. They ship from their Harwich, MA warehouses to homeowners, builders, and contractors across the country.

How do I become an American Pro dealer?

American Pro is actively adding stocking dealers on the East Coast for Legacy PVC decking, TrueGrain Deck, PVC porch flooring, InvisiClip, and PVC mouldings. Independent lumberyards and building-materials distributors can contact us at 1-877-442-6776 or sales@americanprobp.com to discuss territory and stocking programs.

Can I buy American Pro decking online?

Yes. Stonewood Products fulfills Legacy PVC decking orders online at 1-855-786-9663 or stonewoodproducts.com. For factory-direct quotes on TrueGrain Deck, InvisiClip, and PVC mouldings, contact American Pro at 1-877-442-6776 or through the contact form.
