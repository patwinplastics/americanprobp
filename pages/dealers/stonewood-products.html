Questions

Working with Stonewood.

What Cape Cod builders and homeowners ask about the Stonewood dealer relationship.

What decking does Stonewood Products carry?

Stonewood Products carries American Pro Legacy PVC decking in Driftwood and Khaki as their in-stock colors, with the full six-color Legacy PVC line available by special order. Alongside Legacy PVC they stock cedar, mahogany, ipe, and fir. Legacy PVC is available at both the Harwich and Mashpee stores.

What are Stonewood Products' hours?

Both Stonewood Products locations are open Monday through Friday 7 AM to 5 PM, Saturday 7:30 AM to 4:30 PM, and Sunday 9 AM to 2:30 PM. Holiday hours may vary; call ahead.

Does Stonewood deliver decking off-Cape?

Yes. Stonewood delivers American Pro Legacy PVC decking across Cape Cod, Southeastern Massachusetts (Plymouth County, Bristol County, South Shore), and to the Islands via Woods Hole and Hyannis ferry routes. Call 508-430-5020 to schedule regional or island delivery.

Can I order Stonewood Products online?

Yes. Stonewood offers national online ordering at 1-855-786-9663 and through stonewoodproducts.com. For Cape Cod and Southeastern Massachusetts orders, calling either the Harwich (508-430-5020) or Mashpee (508-477-9950) store direct is usually faster.
