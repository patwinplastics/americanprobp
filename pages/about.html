Our Story

Fifty years of
quiet American manufacturing.

American Pro is the building products line of Patwin Plastics. We've been extruding PVC profiles in the United States since 1972 — and we make every American Pro product the same way.

Quality & Innovation

The standard is
the same standard.

When it comes to quality and innovation in the building materials industry, there's no company that can compare to Patwin Plastics. For over fifty years, we've been manufacturing products that are both reliable and durable.

Our line of American Pro Building Products is no exception. These profiles are made from UV-stabilized cellular PVC, which means they won't split, crack, splinter, or rot.

So whether you're a design engineer looking for a product that will stand the test of time, or a contractor who needs a product that's easy to work with, or a homeowner who wants their porch to look like it did the day it was built — Patwin is your go-to source.

What we believe

Most of what we make never carries our name. It goes into the windows, doors, decks, and porches of homes across all fifty states. American Pro is what we ship under our own banner: the products we'd put on our own houses.

We don't chase trends. We extrude precision profiles, ship them on time, and stand behind every linear foot.
