Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect the information you share with us.

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how American Pro Building Products ("American Pro," "we," "us," or "our"), a brand of Patwin Plastics, Inc., collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit americanprobp.com (the "Site"), submit one of our forms, or otherwise interact with us. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described below.

This policy is provided for general informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice. Your specific rights and our obligations may vary by your state or country of residence.

Information we collect

Information you give us

When you fill out a form on our Site, such as a contact, quote, or sample request, you may provide:

  • Your name and company or business name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Mailing or project address and the location you are working in
  • Any details you choose to include in a message field, such as project scope, product interest, or decking volume

Information collected automatically

When you visit the Site, certain information is collected automatically through your browser and similar technologies, including:

  • Your IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages you view, links you click, and how you arrived at the Site
  • Approximate location derived from your IP address, used to tailor regional content such as where-to-buy information

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries, quote requests, and sample requests
  • To ship samples and product literature you request
  • To connect you with the right American Pro or Patwin Plastics contact, distributor, or dealer
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site
  • To comply with our legal obligations and protect against fraud or misuse

Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar browser technologies to operate the Site and remember your preferences, such as dismissing a regional notice. These technologies help the Site function and let us understand, in aggregate, how it is used. Most web browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings. If you disable cookies, some features of the Site may not function as intended.

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to operate our business, including with:

  • Service providers who help us run the Site and process form submissions. Our website forms are processed and delivered to us through Formspree, which handles the transmission of your submission to our team.
  • Distributors and dealers, where connecting you with a local seller is necessary to respond to your request or fulfill a sample or quote inquiry.
  • Our parent company, Patwin Plastics, Inc., and affiliated brands, where doing so helps us respond to you or operate our business.
  • Legal and safety recipients, when we are required to share information by law, legal process, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

Data retention

We keep the information you provide for as long as needed to respond to your inquiry, fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, and meet our legal and business requirements. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify it.

Your choices and rights

You may contact us at any time to ask what personal information we hold about you, to request a correction, or to ask us to delete it. To unsubscribe from any communications, follow the opt-out instructions in the message or contact us directly.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under laws such as the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) or similar state privacy laws, including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, the right to correct inaccurate information, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below and we will respond as required by applicable law.

Children's privacy

Our Site is intended for a general business and adult audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Third-party links

The Site may link to third-party websites and services, such as our deck visualizer, distributor and dealer sites, social media pages, and affiliated brand sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
