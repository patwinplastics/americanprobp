Where to buy American Pro on the East Coast. Two trusted partners, one phone call.

By American Pro May 6, 2026 3 min read

We extrude in Linden, New Jersey. Most of our customers are not. The way our PVC decking, porch flooring, mouldings, and InvisiClip get to a job site is through the dealer or distributor closest to the project. That network is how our material moves, how it gets stocked locally, and how a contractor can pick it up the same week.

Two partners are doing that work for us on the East Coast right now. If you are spec writing, framing a job, or just trying to figure out who carries American Pro near you, start here.

Western New York and the Finger Lakes

Genesee Reserve Supply

Two-step wholesale distributor for porch flooring

Genesee Reserve Supply is a Rochester-based two-step wholesale distributor that has supplied independent lumber dealers across Western New York and the Finger Lakes since 1952. They stock American Pro porch flooring at both branches and route product through their dealer network.

Serving the trade since 1952.

Cape Cod and Southeastern Massachusetts

Authorized dealer for Legacy PVC decking

Stonewood Products is a Harwich-based wholesale building materials supplier serving homeowners, builders, and landscapers since 1997. They stock American Pro Legacy PVC decking at the Harwich flagship and the Mashpee store, and they ship nationally through their online team.
