Welcome Stonewood Products. Our newest Cape Cod dealer.

By American Pro July 23, 2026 4 min read

We are glad to welcome Stonewood Products as the newest authorized dealer for American Pro. Stonewood now stocks our Legacy PVC decking at both of its Cape Cod stores, which means the material we extrude in Linden, New Jersey is on the floor and ready to pick up on the Cape.

Stonewood is a family-run wholesale building materials supplier that has served Cape builders, landscapers, and homeowners since 1997. They know coastal construction, they know what holds up in salt air and freeze-thaw, and they were the right fit for a PVC deck board built for exactly that environment. Adding them to our dealer network gives Cape Cod, the South Shore, Southeastern Massachusetts, and the Islands a local source for American Pro decking.

Two colors, chosen for the Cape

Stonewood stocks American Pro Legacy PVC decking in Driftwood and Khaki, the two tones that fit the Cape's grey-shingle and weathered-cedar palette best. Both are solid, through-the-board cellular PVC with a low-gloss wood-grain surface, so they will not rot, splinter, or wick moisture the way wood does.

  • Driftwood
    Light grey wood-grain
  • Khaki
    Warm tan

Both colors are carried in inventory at Harwich and Mashpee in the 1x5.5 profile, available in 12, 16, and 20 foot lengths, and backed by a 25-year residential warranty. If you want to see the grain and color in person before you order, request free American Pro hand samples and compare them on your own project first.
