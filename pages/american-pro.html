The Brand

American Pro.
The building products line of Patwin Plastics.

American Pro Building Products is the American-made cellular PVC line from Patwin Plastics, a family-run extruder that has been quietly supplying the construction industry with precision PVC profiles since 1971. American Pro is what we ship under our own banner: PVC decking, porch flooring, and architectural mouldings engineered to outlast the wood they replace, manufactured in Linden, New Jersey.

Common questions about
American Pro.

What is American Pro?

American Pro, formally American Pro Building Products, is the building products brand of Patwin Plastics, a family-run PVC extruder founded in 1971 and based in Linden, New Jersey. American Pro manufactures cellular PVC decking, porch flooring, and architectural mouldings.

Where is American Pro made?

Every American Pro profile is designed, compounded, and extruded by Patwin Plastics at 2300 East Linden Avenue in Linden, New Jersey. American Pro is American-made and American-owned. No offshore extrusion, no contract manufacturing.

Who owns American Pro?

American Pro is owned by Patwin Plastics, Inc., a family-run polymer extrusion company founded in 1971 by Thomas Hannen and run today by his sons Timothy Hannen and Thomas Hannen Jr., with the next generation already coming up through the business.

What products does American Pro make?

The American Pro line covers TrueGrain Deck™ wood-grained PVC decking; Legacy PVC Decking, the original solid-color cellular PVC deck board; InvisiClip™, the hidden fastener system for grooved PVC decking; porch flooring in tongue-and-groove cellular PVC; and a full catalog of PVC architectural mouldings including crowns, casings, sills, and brick mould.

Where can I buy American Pro products?

American Pro ships through a national network of independent lumber dealers and distributors. Genesee Reserve Supply stocks American Pro porch flooring across Western New York. Stonewood Products carries Legacy PVC decking on Cape Cod and ships nationally. Call 1-877-442-6776 or email sales@americanprobp.com to be pointed to a dealer in your region.

Is American Pro the same as Patwin Plastics?

American Pro Building Products is the consumer-facing brand. Patwin Plastics is the parent manufacturer. The same plant, the same compound, the same family. American Pro is what Patwin ships under its own banner; the rest of what Patwin extrudes goes to OEMs under the customer's name.
