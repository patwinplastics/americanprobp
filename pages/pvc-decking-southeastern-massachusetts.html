Why is PVC decking a good choice for coastal Southeastern Massachusetts?

Buzzards Bay, Cape Cod Bay, and the South Shore all have coastal salt-air exposure that greys and splinters wood over time. Cellular PVC has no wood fiber to rot, no wood pores for salt to work into, and no organic content to promote mold. American Pro Legacy PVC decking is UV-stabilized and backed by a 25-year residential warranty against splitting, flaking, rotting, and fade beyond 5 Delta E.
