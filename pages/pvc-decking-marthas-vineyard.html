Where can I buy PVC decking on Martha's Vineyard?

American Pro Legacy PVC decking is supplied to Martha's Vineyard through Stonewood Products on Cape Cod. Stonewood's Mashpee store at 544 Route 130 (508-477-9950) is the closest to the Woods Hole ferry terminal, and their Harwich flagship (508-430-5020) can also arrange freight. Both locations stock American Pro Legacy PVC decking in six colors.

Can you deliver PVC decking to Vineyard Haven, Oak Bluffs, or Edgartown?

Yes. Stonewood Products schedules ferry-friendly deliveries from their Mashpee store to Vineyard Haven, Oak Bluffs, and Edgartown, and can coordinate with Vineyard builders on Chilmark, West Tisbury, and Aquinnah projects. Call the Mashpee store at 508-477-9950 to arrange.

Is cellular PVC decking a good choice for Martha's Vineyard?

Cellular PVC is one of the best decking materials for Martha's Vineyard. Vineyard homes take salt spray, sun, and wind for months, exactly what rots wood and greys pressure-treated pine. American Pro Legacy PVC decking is solid-color cellular PVC with a 25-year residential warranty against splitting, flaking, and rotting, and against fade beyond 5 Delta E.

What decking colors work on a Vineyard shingle-style home?

American Pro Legacy PVC decking comes in six solid-color finishes with embossed wood-grain texture that read well against weathered cedar shingles: driftwood greys, warm reds, and neutral browns. Request free samples to compare colors against your Vineyard home before you order.
