Yes. Stonewood Products coordinates Steamship Authority freight from the Hyannis terminal to Nantucket, and works with island-based contractors on delivery scheduling for Siasconset, Madaket, and jobs across the island. Call 508-430-5020 (Harwich) or 508-477-9950 (Mashpee) to arrange.

Why is cellular PVC decking the right pick for Nantucket?

Nantucket sits 30 miles offshore in the open Atlantic, and Nantucket decks take relentless salt spray, wind, and sun. Wood rots, greys, and splinters under that punishment. Cellular PVC has no wood fiber to break down, no wood pores for salt to work into, and no organic content to promote mold or algae. American Pro Legacy PVC decking is UV-stabilized and backed by a 25-year residential warranty.

What decking colors match Nantucket cedar-shingle homes?

American Pro Legacy PVC decking comes in six solid-color finishes with embossed wood-grain texture. The driftwood and warm-grey tones pair especially well with weathered cedar-shingle Nantucket architecture. Request free samples to compare colors on-site before you order.
