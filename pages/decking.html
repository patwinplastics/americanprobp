Inside every TrueGrain board

Three layers,
one extrusion line.

Layer 01

TrueGrain laminated surface

Decorative exterior foil bonded to the cap. Deep variegated grain you can feel under bare feet.

Layer 02

Protective ASA cap

Weatherable ASA capstock seals the board on all four sides. Resists fade, stain, and the elements.

Layer 03

Cellular PVC core

Moisture-resistant cellular PVC with 30% repurposed polymer content. Won't rot, won't absorb water.

Original line

Standard PVC Decking. The workhorse.

Solid-color UV-stabilized PVC with a subtle wood-grain texture. The board that built our reputation. Same compound chemistry as TrueGrain Deck, finished in classic tones. Top-fasten or hidden-clip, residential or commercial.

Two profiles

Solid Edge or
Grooved.

Profile

Solid Edge

Traditional square-edge boards. Top-fastened with screws and color-matched plugs. The most flexible install for picture framing, breaker boards, and angled patterns.

Profile

Grooved

Grooved boards for use with hidden fastener clips. Cleaner finished surface with no visible screws across the field. Ideal for straight runs over standard joist framing.

Intertek wind uplift testing.

American Pro grooved decking installed with our InvisiClip™ hidden fastener system was tested by Intertek Building & Construction, an IAS-accredited TL-144 / ISO/IEC 17025 lab, under ICC-ES™ AC174 Section 4.1.4 using ASTM E330 methods. Three specimens reached an average sustained uplift of 292 psf at a 10 second hold and an average ultimate uplift of 306 psf before clip release.

Standard PVC warranty

25 years residential.
10 years commercial.

Patwin Plastics warrants every Standard PVC deck and porch board against splitting, flaking, rotting, or structural defects from manufacturing. Boards are designed to resist fading and will not fade more than 5 Delta E (CIE) units after exposure to UV and weathering. The stain warranty covers permanent staining from food and beverage when cleaned within one week of exposure.

TrueGrain Deck is covered by a separate 25-year limited residential foil warranty issued through RENOLIT. Full terms, exclusions, and claim procedure on the warranty page. Manufacturer: Patwin Plastics, Inc., 2300 E Linden Ave, Linden NJ 07036.
