How we compute it

Board geometry
1" × 5.5" actual board face with a recommended 3/16" gap. Effective coverage per board = 5.6875".
Standard lengths
12 ft, 16 ft, 20 ft. We pick the length that minimizes waste, no spliced rows under 20 ft span.
Spans over 20 ft
One 20 ft board plus the smallest standard board that covers the remainder, single-overage applied once.
Joists
Joist count = floor(span / spacing) + 1, plus a doubled rim joist on each end. Joist length matches the perpendicular deck dimension.
Fasteners
Face-screw + plug system, 2 screws and 2 plugs per board-to-joist intersection. Switch to hidden clips with your dealer if you prefer.
Overage
7.5% applied once to total board length. Costs derive from purchased-length, not theoretical-length, so pricing reflects what you actually buy.
Defaults
Legacy PVC $6.00/ft, TrueGrain Deck $8.00/ft, PT 2×8 joist $1.65/ft, $0.09 each on screws and plugs (works out to $36 per 100 sq ft fastener), labor $15/sq ft.
What we don't include
Footings, beams, ledger flashing, railing, stairs, permits. These vary too much by jurisdiction and design to put in a generic calc, talk to your contractor.
