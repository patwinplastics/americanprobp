Why It Matters

Material that
outlives the trend.

Wood looks beautiful the day it's installed. Five summers in, it's another story. Our cellular PVC is engineered to look exactly the same on day one and decade two.

UV-stabilized

Color-fast formulation engineered to fade no more than 5 DeltaE under sunlight and weathering, backed by a 25-year stain and fade warranty.

Won't rot or splinter

Closed-cell PVC has no moisture path for rot, no grain for splinters, and no food for termites. It just stays the way you installed it.

Workable like wood

Cuts, routes, and fastens with standard tools. Holds paint beautifully. Your installer doesn't need a manual, they need a saw.

American-made

Designed and extruded by Patwin Plastics in Linden, NJ. Family-run, American-owned, and shipping precision PVC profiles since 1971.

Our Story

Fifty years of
American extrusion.

American Pro is the building products line of Patwin Plastics, a family-run extruder that has been quietly supplying the construction industry with precision PVC profiles since 1971.

Most of what we make never carries our name. It goes into the windows, doors, decks, and porches of homes across all fifty states. American Pro is what we ship under our own banner: the products we'd put on our own houses.
